ABC Technologies Employee Handbook

Company Name:
ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd.

Working Hours:
Employees are expected to work from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.

Annual Leave:
Employees receive 18 paid annual leave days every calendar year.

Sick Leave:
Employees are entitled to 12 sick leave days per year.

Work From Home:
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Probation Period:
The probation period lasts for 6 months.

Notice Period:
Employees must serve a 60-day notice period before leaving the company.

Salary Payment:
Salaries are credited on the last working day of every month.

Health Insurance:
The company provides medical insurance coverage of ₹5,00,000 for employees and their immediate family.

Laptop Policy:
Each employee receives a company laptop that must be returned upon resignation.

Travel Policy:
Employees traveling for business are eligible for reimbursement of travel, hotel, and meal expenses.

Performance Review:
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year in June and December.

Office Location:
The headquarters is located in Hyderabad, Telangana.

Support Email:
Employees can contact hr@abctech.com for HR-related queries.
